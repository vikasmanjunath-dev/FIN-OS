FINAL PROTOCOL

Your Financial Order of Operations

Don't jump to Step 5 (Investing) before fixing Step 3 (Emergency Fund).

1

Income Clarity

✅ DONE

Know exactly what hits your bank account vs what is "CTC".

Rule: You cannot manage what you do not measure.
2

Expense Control

📍 CURRENT

The Leak: "Chalta Hai" spending on weekends.

The Fix: Track "Needs" vs "Wants" for 30 days. Kill 2 subscriptions you don't use.

Target: Save at least 20% of Income.
3

Emergency Fund

🔒 LOCKED

Before buying stocks, keep 6 Months of Expenses in a Liquid Fund/FD.

Why? So you don't sell your investments when life hits you.

4

Insurance (Protection)

🔒 LOCKED

Health: 5L Base + 20L Super Top-up.

Life: Term Insurance (20x Annual Income). Do not mix insurance with investment (ULIP).

5

Investing (Growth)

🔒 LOCKED

Now you are ready to beat inflation.

  • Start Nifty 50 SIP.
  • Buy Gold (SGB).
  • Explore Stocks.
