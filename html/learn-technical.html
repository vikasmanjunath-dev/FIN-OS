⬇ INITIATE CROWD SCAN
CONCEPT 01: THE CROWD

You are at a Railway Station 🚉

You don't know who these people are. You don't know their jobs. But you can tell everything just by watching their movement.

👨‍💼👩‍⚕️👷

The "Who" (Ignored)

"Is he a doctor? Is she an engineer?"
IRRELEVANT right now.

The Insight: If everyone is running towards Platform 4, you know the train is coming. You don't need to check the timetable. Price Movement = Crowd Running.
CONCEPT 02: THE SIGNAL

The Traffic Light Logic 🚦

Markets move *before* the news. Why wait for the newspaper when you can see the signal?

🟢

Momentum

Price is rising fast. Volume is high.
"The crowd is confident."

🟡

Indecision

Price is stuck. Candles are small.
"The crowd is confused."

🔴

Fear

Price dropping. Panic selling.
"The crowd is running away."

Desi Example: The traffic light is green, but you see cars slowing down ahead. Do you keep speeding? No. You prepare to brake. Technical Analysis = Seeing the brake lights.
CONCEPT 03: THE SCOREBOARD

The Chart is a Visual Diary 🏏

A chart is just a scoreboard of Buyers vs Sellers. It shows who is winning *right now*.

BUYERS
Dominance

"Aggressive. Buying at any price."

SELLERS
Pressure

"Weak. Waiting for higher price."

Cricket Logic: You don't know the batter's technique. But if the scoreboard says "50 runs in 20 balls", you know he is dominating. Chart = The Match Scoreboard.
CONCEPT 04: EXECUTION

Practical Application

We don't use this to predict the future. We use it to time our actions.

ACTION 01

Better Entry

Don't buy when price is vertical (FOMO). Buy when it cools down.

🛒 Sabzi Mandi: Don't buy during peak rush. Go early morning.
ACTION 02

Clean Exit

Sell when momentum slows, not after the crash.

🚪 The Wedding: Leave before the traffic jam starts at the gate.
ACTION 03

Stop Loss

Accepting a small mistake before it becomes a disaster.

🚑 The Leak: Fixing a pipe leak today vs replacing the whole wall next month.
