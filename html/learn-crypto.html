Crypto Protocol

Money without Masters. Trust without People.

LEVEL 01: NOOB

The "Shared Ledger" Concept

Imagine a Google Sheet that tracks who owns what money. But:

  • ❌ No Admin (No Google, No Bank).
  • ✅ Everyone has a copy.
  • ✅ To add a row, everyone must agree (Consensus).
  • 🔒 Once written, it can NEVER be deleted.

This is Blockchain. Bitcoin is just the first app running on this system.

LEVEL 02: MECHANIC

Bitcoin vs Ethereum (Gold vs Oil)

🥇 Bitcoin (BTC) = Digital Gold

Purpose: Store of Value.

Why: Only 21 Million will ever exist. No government can print more. It protects wealth against inflation.

🛢️ Ethereum (ETH) = Digital Oil

Purpose: Fuel for the Global Computer.

Why: You need ETH to pay "Gas Fees" to run apps, games, and banks (DeFi) built on the Ethereum network.

LEVEL 03: PRO

Tokenomics (Don't Get Scammed)

Before buying any "Altcoin", check these two numbers:

Market Cap

Current Price x Circulating Supply

"What it's worth today"

FDV

Current Price x TOTAL Supply

"What it's worth if all tokens unlock"

⚠️ If Market Cap is $1M but FDV is $100M, run away. Massive inflation is coming.

☠️ GOD MODE: Self-Custody

"Not your Keys, Not your Coins."

If your crypto is on an exchange (Binance/Coinbase), you don't own it. The bank owns it. If they go bankrupt (like FTX), you lose everything.

The Fix: Use a Hardware Wallet (Ledger/Trezor). Become your own bank.
