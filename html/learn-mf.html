MF Protocol

Outsourcing your wealth management. The good, the bad, and the hidden fees.

LEVEL 01: NOOB

The Bus Driver Analogy

Driving a car (Stocks) requires skill. If you don't know how to drive, you take a Bus (Mutual Fund).

The Fund Manager is the driver. You and 10,000 others are passengers. You pool your money, and the driver decides the route.

LEVEL 02: MECHANIC

NAV & Units

NAV (Net Asset Value): The price of one ticket on the bus.

AUM (Assets Under Management): The total cash the bus is carrying. Warning: If AUM is too huge (₹50,000 Cr+), the bus becomes slow and hard to turn (hard to find new stock ideas).

LEVEL 03: PRO

Active vs Passive

The Harsh Truth (SPIVA Report)

Active Fund: Manager tries to beat Nifty. Charges 1-2% fee.
Passive Fund: Simply copies Nifty. Charges 0.2% fee.

Fact: 80% of Active Funds FAIL to beat Passive Funds over a 10-year period.

☠️ GOD MODE: The "Regular Plan" Scam

Never buy a "Regular" Mutual Fund from a bank or agent.

Regular Plan: Includes 1% commission to the agent every single year from your wealth.

Direct Plan: Zero commission. That 1% compounds. Over 20 years, a Direct plan gives you ₹30-40 Lakhs MORE on a standard SIP portfolio.
